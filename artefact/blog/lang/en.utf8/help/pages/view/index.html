View Blog

When viewing a Blog you will see your postings with Drafts listed first followed by Published posts, both in descending date order.  10 postings are listed per page and a list of any files that are attached to a post are displayed.

You can Publish Draft postings from this screen as well as Edit or Delete both Draft and Published postings.  Deleting a Blog post with an attached file will not remove the file from your Files area.
