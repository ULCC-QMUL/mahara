My Blogs

A web log or Blog is journal-like tool that allows you to record your thoughts and experiences. You can allow other users to post comments on your Blog allowing you to create a dialogue with your readers.

While a Blog itself is considered an Artefact, so are any individual Blog postings you create. This means both Blogs and Blog posts may be added to a View.

Your Blogs area lists of all of your current Blogs. Click on the name of a Blog to add a new posting or edit the Blog settings. To create a new Blog select Add Blog.
