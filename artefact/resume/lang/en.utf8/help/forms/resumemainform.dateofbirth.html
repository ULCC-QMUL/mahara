Date of Birth

You can either enter in the required date using the below format or use the Calendar to select your Date of Birth

Date Format:
YYYY/MM/DD
(Year/Month/Day)

For example: 1978/06/02

Calendar
Click the << >> buttons to move through the year list or click and hold either of the << >> buttons to open a drop down menu of available years.

Click the < > buttons to cycle through the months or click and hold either of the buttons to open a drop down menu of all months

Click on the time fields to move up through the hours and minutes or Shift-click to move down through the hours and minutes. Click and drag your mouse to move increase or decrease the time quickly.
