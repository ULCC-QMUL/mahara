Profile Icons

The Profile Icons area allows you to upload images to use within your Profile and Views. Your Icons may be a picture of you or an avatar image of your choice.

You may have up to 5 icons loaded to your Profiles Icons area at once. Images you upload need to be between at least 16x16 pixels and will be displayed resized so that the longest edge is 100 pixels long.

Your default image will appear on your Profile page.
