Email address

Although the email address given during registration is your primary contact address, you may have several email addresses listed on the site and use any validated address as a contact address within a View. 

Add new addresses using the Add Email button.  Once you have saved your Profile a message will be sent to the new email address.  This message will contain a link which once followed, will confirm your email address is valid.  This confirmation email is valid for 24 hours only.  After this time you will need to repeat the Add Email process.
