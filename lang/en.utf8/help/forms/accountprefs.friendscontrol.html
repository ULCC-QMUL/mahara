Friends Control

Use Friends Control to choose whether other users on the site may add you to their Friends list.  If you are added to someone’s Friends list they are automatically added to your list.  When you remove someone from your Friends list you are also removed from their list. 

Nobody may add me as a friend
No user on the site may add you to their Friends list.

New friends require my authorisation
People must request your authorisation in order to add you to their Friends list.  Once you have approved a request they will be added as a Friend.

New friends are automatically authorised
Anyone may add you to their Friends list at any time.
