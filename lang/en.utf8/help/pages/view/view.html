Accessing artefacts

Place feedback

Public feedback may be seen by other users with access to the view, while private feedback may only be read by the view owner. A view owner can make private feedback public.

Tutors can include a file with their feedback where the view has been submitted to them for assessment.

Report objectionable material

You can report material you believe to be objectionable to the Site Administrator.

Print

The Print option will allow you print a printer friendly version of the view.

Add View to Watchlist

If a view is on your watchlist you receive notification of changes.
