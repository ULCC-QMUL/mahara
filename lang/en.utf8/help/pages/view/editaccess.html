Edit View Acces

In Edit View Acces you can assign View access to any of your Groups, available Communities and Individuals for a set period of time.

You can search for specific users, Groups and Communities or choose a user from the list given. Click Add against the selected user and enter a start and finish date for their access in the box to the right. If you do not enter in a date here, access for this user will default to the start and end dates of the View itself.

Depending on the site settings you may also be able to give View access to all logged in users, the general public or those people listed in your Friends area.
